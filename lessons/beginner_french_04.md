# Lesson 4: Getting Around — Directions and Transport

## Learning Objectives
- Ask where something is in French
- Understand basic directions
- Navigate the metro and train station
- Ask for help finding your way

## Steps
- [ ] Step 1: Learn to ask where things are: "Où est...?" (Where is...?) and "Où sont les toilettes?" (Where are the toilets?)
- [ ] Step 2: Learn key places: la gare (train station), le métro, l'arrêt de bus (bus stop), le centre-ville (city centre), la pharmacie, la plage (beach)
- [ ] Step 3: Learn directions: à gauche (left), à droite (right), tout droit (straight ahead), près (near), loin (far)
- [ ] Step 4: Learn transport phrases: "Un billet pour... s'il vous plaît" (A ticket to... please), "Quelle ligne?" (Which line?), "C'est quelle direction?" (Which direction is it?)
- [ ] Step 5: Learn to understand responses: "C'est par là" (It's that way), "Tournez à gauche" (Turn left), "C'est juste à côté" (It's right next door)
- [ ] Step 6: Role-play — you're at a train station, buy a ticket, ask which platform, then ask a passerby for directions to your hotel

## Review from previous lessons
- Start with "Excusez-moi" before asking a stranger for directions
- Use "Merci beaucoup" after receiving directions

## Quiz
- [ ] Q1: Ask "Where is the metro station?" in French
- [ ] Q2: Ask for a ticket and say which direction you need
- [ ] Q3: Ask where the toilets are, then say thank you and goodbye
