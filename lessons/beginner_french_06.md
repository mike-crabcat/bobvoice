# Lesson 6: Shopping — Bakery, Market, and Souvenirs

## Learning Objectives
- Buy things at a bakery or market
- Ask about prices and sizes
- Say how many of something you want
- Pay and complete a purchase

## Steps
- [ ] Step 1: Learn bakery items: une baguette, un croissant, une tarte (a tart/pie), du pain (bread), un pain au chocolat
- [ ] Step 2: Learn to request items: "Je voudrais une baguette, s'il vous plaît" and "Donnez-moi..." (Give me...)
- [ ] Step 3: Learn quantities: "un" (one), "deux" (two), "trois" (three), "un kilo de..." (a kilo of...), "demi" (half)
- [ ] Step 4: Learn to ask about prices: "C'est combien?" (How much is it?), "Je paie par carte" (I'll pay by card), "En espèces" (in cash)
- [ ] Step 5: Learn market phrases: "C'est local?" (Is it local?), "Je regarde, merci" (I'm just looking, thanks), "C'est trop cher" (It's too expensive), "Vous avez...?" (Do you have...?)
- [ ] Step 6: Role-play — go to a bakery, order a baguette and two croissants, ask the price, pay and thank the baker

## Review from previous lessons
- Use "Bonjour" when entering any shop
- Use "Je voudrais" from Lesson 2 for ordering
- Use "Merci, au revoir" when leaving

## Quiz
- [ ] Q1: At a bakery, order a baguette and a pain au chocolat
- [ ] Q2: At a market stall, ask if something is local and ask the price
- [ ] Q3: Say "I'm just looking" and "it's too expensive" in French
