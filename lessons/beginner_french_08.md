# Lesson 8: Likes, Dislikes, and Making Choices

## Learning Objectives
- Express what you like and don't like
- Make choices and give simple opinions
- Ask others what they think
- Agree and disagree politely

## Steps
- [ ] Step 1: Learn "J'aime" (I like) and "Je n'aime pas" (I don't like) with food and activities
- [ ] Step 2: Learn "J'adore" (I love) and "Je préfère" (I prefer) for expressing stronger opinions
- [ ] Step 3: Learn to ask: "Tu aimes...?" (Do you like...?) and "Qu'est-ce que tu préfères?" (What do you prefer?)
- [ ] Step 4: Learn to agree and disagree: "D'accord" (OK/agreed), "Bien sûr" (of course), "Peut-être" (maybe), "Pas du tout" (not at all)
- [ ] Step 5: Learn to choose: "Je vais prendre celui-ci" (I'll take this one), "L'autre, s'il vous plaît" (The other one, please), "C'est bon" (It's good)
- [ ] Step 6: Role-play — at an ice cream shop, look at flavours, say what you like and don't like, choose one, and ask a friend what they want

## Review from previous lessons
- Use café and restaurant vocabulary from Lessons 2 and 3
- Use "Je voudrais" for ordering
- Use quantities from Lesson 6

## Quiz
- [ ] Q1: Say "I love chocolate ice cream" and "I don't like vanilla" in French
- [ ] Q2: Ask a friend "Do you prefer coffee or tea?" in French
- [ ] Q3: At a shop, say "I'll take this one" and "it's too expensive"
