# Lesson 1: Greetings and Essentials

## Learning Objectives
- Say hello and goodbye in French
- Say please, thank you, and excuse me
- Say yes and no
- Ask if someone speaks English
- Introduce yourself by name

## Steps
- [ ] Step 1: Listen to and repeat "Bonjour" — the standard way to say hello
- [ ] Step 2: Listen to and repeat "Salut" — the informal way to say hi, and "Au revoir" — goodbye
- [ ] Step 3: Listen to and repeat "Merci" (thank you) and "S'il vous plaît" (please)
- [ ] Step 4: Listen to and repeat "Excusez-moi" (excuse me) and "Pardon" (sorry)
- [ ] Step 5: Listen to and repeat "Oui" (yes) and "Non" (no)
- [ ] Step 6: Learn to ask "Parlez-vous anglais?" (Do you speak English?) and introduce yourself with "Je m'appelle..." (My name is...)
